<h2 id="overview">OverviewğŸ“Œ</h2>

<p>
The Geoportal Perkimtan project was built with the goal of delivering a responsive and accessible geospatial platform that supports urban development planning in Batam City. Key objectives include:
</p>

<ol>
  <li>
    Enhancing the quality of inventory, management, and presentation of housing and settlement data, overcoming spatial and temporal limitations to deliver easy and accurate access to information.
  </li>
  <li>
    Developing an information system accessible by both internal and external stakeholders of the Department of Housing and Settlement, enabling easier search and retrieval of data.
  </li>
  <li>
    Consolidating data from various aspects of housing and settlements across Batam City, presenting measurable insights to support government programs and development planning.
  </li>
  <li>
    Promoting transparency and encouraging public participation through crowdsourced data contribution features and enabling data-driven decision making.
  </li>
</ol>
